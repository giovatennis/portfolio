About Me

Passionate educator and researcher dedicated to improving computer science education through innovative game-based learning approaches

Nice to meet you! I have been teaching as a lecturer of Computer Science for the past few years, and have got to learn a lot along the way!

I am currently pursuing my Ph.D. at Auburn University, where my research focuses on improving CS students' computational thinking through game-based techniques.

My approach combines academic research with practical teaching experience, creating engaging learning environments that help students develop strong computational thinking skills.
